Why do we Call Ourselves the Glasses Gang?

We call ourselves the Glasses Gang because one day, the fantasy member of the group noticed that we all wore glasses, and they we're all worn almost 24/7. He came up with the name, "Glasses Gang," to group us together. This is when the Glasses Gang was started.
